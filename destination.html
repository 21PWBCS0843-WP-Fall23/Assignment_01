Swat Destination Guide

Places to Visit

Swat Valley is a beautiful and captivating valley in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan. It is known for its stunning scenery, lush forests, crystal-clear lakes, and snow-capped mountains. Swat Valley is also a place of rich history and culture, with numerous ancient ruins and historical sites.

  • Swat Museum



  • Kalam Valley


  • Malam Jabba Ski Resort


  • Hindu Kush Mountains


  • Mahodand Lake


  • Bashigram Lake

Things to Do

  • Take a boat trip on the Swat River

  • Go hiking in the Hindu Kush Mountains

  • Visit the Swat Museum

  • Go skiing or snowboarding at Malam Jabba Ski Resort

  • Visit the Kalam Valley

  • Visit Mahodand Lake

  • Visit Bashigram Lake

  • Try the local cuisine

  • Visit the local markets

Local Tips

  • Be aware of the political situation in the region.

  • Dress respectfully.

  • Be prepared for the weather.

  • Be aware of your surroundings.

  • Try the local cuisine.

  • Be sure to bargain when shopping.

  • Learn a few basic Pashto phrases.
